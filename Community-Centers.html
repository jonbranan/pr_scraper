Buildings & Centers

The San Antonio Parks and Recreation Department operates a myriad of facilities designed to meet your recreational needs. These include more than 20 community centers where sports, dance, fitness and variety of other recreational programs are offered. 

Also available:

  • Fitness Center
  • Four Adult and Senior Centers
  • Urban ecology center
  • Sunken Garden Theater
  • Dance Studio
  • Club House

There are a wide variety of facilities suitable for special occasions such as birthday parties, family reunions, and wedding receptions. Find out how to reserve a facility by visiting our reservations page.

These aren't simply buildings, they serve as the gateway to fun, fitness and friendship for those who enter. Join us!
